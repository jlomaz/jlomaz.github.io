Jill's Background

Some details about the designer

Jill Lomaz

User Experience Designer

I have been involved in user-centered design for almost two decades. I've had the privilege of designing for a variety of industries including nonprofit, telecom, government, and biomedical sciences. My distinguished skills as a designer have earned numerous awards and accolades, and my work has been featured on jumbotrons in Times Square. Throughout my career, I've had considerable experience as Section 508 Subject Matter Expert, and am often hired to lead entire organizations in their accessibility efforts.
